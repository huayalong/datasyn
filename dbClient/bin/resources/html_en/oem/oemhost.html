The maintenance of the database is divided into "physical backup and recovery" and "scheduling" the two modules, users can be assisted to complete the following steps:
 
  • Physical backup of the database: database of three types of powers of the physical back-up operations, are as follows: full backup, incremental backup and differential backup.
  • Physical restoration of the database: the user can back up from the inside (that is, the database server with magical powers has been in existence for backup) or external backup (that is, a device of the backup file) to restore the database.
  • Scheduling Operations Management: Scheduling task management and scheduling program to implement and often repeated task scheduling.
